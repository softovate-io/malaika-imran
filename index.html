Hi! I’m Malaika Imran, Founder and Creative Director of Alvanta Talent — a human-centric Influencer Talent Marketing Studio driven entirely by my creative direction. I started Alvanta to help brands cut through the noise of the influencer economy and create collaborations that are authentic, transparent, and truly impactful. With over 2 years of comprehensive experience, I oversee & direct every stage of the process—from brand pitching and talent scouting to campaign strategy and execution—so my clients never have to worry about chasing influencers or juggling multiple partnerships.
To date, I’ve delivered 25+ successful brand campaigns, connecting with 800+ creators across Pakistan, the US, UK, and UAE. Every project is executed with precision, creativity, and integrity, ensuring results that speak for themselves.
If you’re looking for someone who can transform your brand vision into meaningful influencer campaigns without the hassle, let’s connect and make it happen.
